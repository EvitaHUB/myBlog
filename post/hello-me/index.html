Hello friend! or the very first post

2020-12-11

“I want to finally go to sleep. Could this work, please, Hugo, please, be my friend!” : this was my first sentence for this blog, after choosing from 7 themes and making 6th versions of Hugo site :)) and fighting with image adding to the site. It still does not work good. I get ones that are in blog post header, but one in the body text do not work…..

OK, one week and 200 hours of youtube later it works :))

The title image now should be photo I took in Lithuania, at top of Šatrija hillfort. Amazing place! Hillfort hunting is one of my favorite things to do. I have visited about 250 in Latvia and Lithuania. Still want to see them all, I`m in the middle of the list at the moment.

This interest led me to interest about maps and map making. I had a map on google maps and then learned a bit QGIS by myself.

The picture below is one visual version of map. I made it learning QGIS and it depicts a layer of Hillfort locations in Latvia. I wish we would have course about GIS in university.
